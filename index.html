Introduction

Blink and You’ll Miss It is a fast-paced mini-game collection, the player will choose their favorite character, and enter his/her story. The mini-games are funny and relaxed, with different genres, perspectives, and control schemes. Each mini-game will last less than 20 seconds. During this short period, the player needs to quickly familiarize themselves with the mini-game, figure out what the win condition is, and after completing it get ready for the next one.

About the Game

Every mini-game has a different way of being played, with dragging and touching the screen being the most common methods. This is because the majority of the games on the market utilize these interactions, so the player will be more familiar with these types of controls. We are experimenting with other controls such as tilting the phone or making use of the other sensors
